# Imerhaba Admin Panel

Administrative panel for managing Mohamed's multi-sector business ecosystem.

## About

This admin panel serves as the central management system for an integrated business ecosystem spanning:

- Real Estate & Investment Development
- Glamping & Prefab Housing Manufacturing
- Tourism & Car Rental Operations
- Consulting & Immigration Advisory
- AI & SaaS Platforms
- Brand Building & Market Connectivity

## Documentation

- [Business Roles & Occupation](docs/BUSINESS_ROLES.md) - Detailed overview of the business structure and roles

## Technical Details

- **Platform**: PHP
- **Version**: See composer.json for specific requirements
- **Server**: Apache with .htaccess configuration
