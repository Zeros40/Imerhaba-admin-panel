# Mohamed's Business Roles & Occupation

## Overview

**Mohamed is a multi-sector entrepreneur building an integrated ecosystem across real estate, tourism, prefab housing, consulting, and AI platforms.**

---

## Role Hierarchy (Top → Down)

### 1. Multi-Sector Entrepreneur

Builds, owns, and runs several interconnected businesses at the same time.

### 2. Business Strategist & System Architect

Designs models, structures, workflows, automation, and growth plans.

### 3. Real Estate & Investment Developer

Buys, sells, develops, and markets land, homes, cottages, domes, and resorts.

### 4. Glamping & Prefab Housing Manufacturer

Creates domes, wooden cottages, modular units, and full eco-resort concepts.

### 5. Tourism & Car Rental Operator

Runs fleets, rentals, airport services, family travel, and seasonal tourism assets.

### 6. Consulting & Immigration Advisor

Guides people to move, invest, and set up companies in Bosnia.

### 7. AI & SaaS Platform Founder

Builds tools, marketplaces, dashboards, automations, and AI-powered systems.

### 8. Brand Builder & Connector

Links Gulf + Balkan markets, builds partnerships, and creates trust ecosystems.

---

## Business Ecosystem

Mohamed's businesses are interconnected, creating synergies across multiple sectors:

- **Real Estate ↔ Tourism**: Properties serve as both investments and tourism accommodations
- **Prefab Housing ↔ Glamping**: Manufacturing capabilities support tourism infrastructure
- **Consulting ↔ Real Estate**: Advisory services facilitate property investments
- **AI/SaaS ↔ All Sectors**: Technology platforms automate and scale all business operations
- **Brand Building**: Creates trust and partnerships across Gulf and Balkan markets
